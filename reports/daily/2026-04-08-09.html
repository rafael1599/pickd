WHAT'S BETTER NOW

●  There's a new Activity Report screen where you fill in a few quick fields, hit Copy, and paste a clean summary into email. It pulls order counts and team activity automatically.
●  A new Projects board lets you move tasks between “Coming Up”, “In Progress”, and “Done” columns. Whatever gets marked done feeds straight into the daily report.
●  Inventory accuracy used to only count items that were manually audited — less than 1% of stock. Now the system also counts items that were physically moved or received, bringing coverage above 20% with zero extra work.
●  Searching for an item that's out of stock no longer shows a confusing full card. Instead, a small “No stock” section tells you what happened and when.
●  The two floating buttons (Add SKU and Download PDF) have been replaced with a single menu button that only opens when you need it.
●  Editing reasons on an order used to be invisible in light mode — now they show up correctly in both themes.
